Experiences
Summer Teaching Intern
I dedicated two months of my summer in 2024 to experience teaching young children at the Cornelia F. Bradford Elementary School in Jersey City, NJ. I worked 30 hours per week, and most days would consist of helping the teacher manage the classroom-- whether guiding students through warm-up activities, helping them solve math problems, or assisting them in writing the alphabet. Aside from teaching, I also had to chaperone the children on field trips and during reccess hours, developing my organizational and leadership skills.
Scratch Club Facilitator
I collaborated with a group of three UW-Madison students during my second semester to facilitate a Scratch Club at Shorewood Hills Elementary School. The club would meet once per week, and we developed weekly lesson plans geared towards addressing deficencies in the students' programming abilities. The club was a mix of self-agency and guided learning, with the first half of the semester relegated towards teaching the students to understand loops, conditionals, and special Scratch features such as event and broadcasting blocks, while the second half was utilized to work on personal projects.
